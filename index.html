The M×L×I Framework

Three dimensions that work together to determine your transitioning capacity:

Motivation

The energy and drive to actually do something about your situation. Not theoretical interest – genuine, actionable motivation.

Learning

Your capacity to acquire new knowledge, skills, and ways of thinking. Bandwidth, cognitive load, and readiness to engage with new material.

Identity

Clarity about who you are beyond your current role. What matters to you, what you value, and what you're willing to change.
