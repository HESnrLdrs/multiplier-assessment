How to Use Your Results

  1. Identify your constraint - This is the dimension marked in red
  2. Review specific questions - Look at which questions in that dimension scored lowest
  3. Create an action plan - Focus on concrete steps to improve that area
  4. Track progress - Retake the assessment every 4 weeks to see improvement
  5. Celebrate wins - Notice when your scores improve and your capacity increases
