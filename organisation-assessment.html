Organisation Assessment

For each statement below, select the number (0–10) that best describes what you observe happening in your organisation, where 0 is "not at all true" and 10 is "completely true".

Your Reading

What you are seeing. Three scores, one per dimension, on a 0 to 10 scale. The dimension with the lowest score is flagged as the constraint, the place where the conditions are most strongly at work. The bands read: 0 to 3, conditions strongly constrain adaptive contribution; 4 to 6, conditions partially support but inconsistently sustain it; 7 to 10, conditions generally support it. The richer interpretive material that explains what each pattern means is under development and will follow when ready.
