Administrator:
How to read this. Every person who has completed a reading appears here under their organisation, using their most recent reading. This view is not gated, so you can see individuals whatever the size of the group. It is the screen to open when you want to discuss someone's result with them. Each dimension shows a light: red below 4, amber 4 to 6.9, green 7 and above. A person's lowest dimension is shaded as their constraint, the one to look at first.
How to read this. Each organisation shows the average Motivation, Learning and Identity across the people in it, using each person's most recent reading. An organisation stays locked until at least four people in it have a reading, so the average is not carried by one or two voices. Bands read: red below 4, amber 4 to 6.9, green 7 and above. The lowest dimension is flagged as the constraint. Organisation names are matched without regard to case.
