Welcome

When you're navigating change, it can be hard to know where you stand. This short check-in helps you understand your current capacity and how transition may be affecting you.

It takes around 5–7 minutes to complete. Your responses are completely confidential. There are no right or wrong answers — just answer honestly based on how things feel for you in this moment.

This is not about judging yourself or fixing anything. It's simply a way to pause, notice where you are, and begin from there.
